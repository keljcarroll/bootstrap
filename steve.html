About My Dad

My dad grew up in Springfield, Virginia along side 11 other brothers and sisters. He is number 11 out of 12 siblings and is the baby boy of the family. My dad attended JMU (GO DUKES) and graduated in 1991. He has been a medical sales representative for the last 25 years. He loves sports, especially golf, and spending time with family. He also loves Fireball. He is the world's greatest dad and I take pride in being a miniature version of him.
